A compact indoor water feature features three stacked hemispherical pools, each level gradually filling as water flows downward from the top. Driven by gravity, water spills over the rim of the uppermost basin once it reaches capacity, cascading into the second layer below. This overflow continues sequentially, with each subsequent pool filling in turn until the lowest reservoir receives the final flow. The water level in each basin rises steadily, maintaining a dynamic equilibrium as inflow matches outflow at each stage. The gentle, continuous movement creates a serene, self-sustaining cycle, where gravity ensures a smooth, uninterrupted descent through all three tiers.
